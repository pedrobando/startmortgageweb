*Copywriting Skill Applied: clarity over cleverness, benefits over features, customer language, one idea per section*

---

## Copywriting Context (Reference — Do Not Publish)

**Page type:** About page
**Primary action:** Book a free planning session → /planning-session
**Audience:** First-time buyers, Spanish-speaking families, self-employed, realtors — anyone evaluating whether to trust START Mortgage
**Problem they're solving:** "Can I trust this company? Are they legit? Will they actually help me?"
**Traffic source:** Organic search, internal navigation from homepage, Google Business Profile
**What visitors already know:** They've seen the homepage or a social post. Now they want to know who's behind this.
**Voice:** Professional but friendly. Confident, not corporate. Warm, not salesy.
**Reading level:** 5th grade or below.
