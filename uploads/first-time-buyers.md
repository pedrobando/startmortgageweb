### SECTION 7: FAQ Accordion (3 Items)
*Inviz Services Section 7*

**Layout:** Elementor Accordion widget. Clean, scannable. Light background.

Common questions

**H2:**
Questions First-Time Buyers Ask

**Q1: How much money do I need to buy my first home?**
A: Less than you might think. FHA loans require as little as 3.5% down. Some conventional programs start at 3% down. If you're a veteran, VA loans offer zero down. You'll also need funds for closing costs, which run about 2-5% of the loan amount. In your planning session, we break down every cost for your situation. All loan amounts are subject to credit approval.

**Q2: What credit score do I need to buy a house in Florida?**
A: For an FHA loan, you can qualify with a credit score as low as 580. Conventional loans typically need 620 or higher. If your score is below these numbers, don't give up — we can review your credit and build a plan to get you mortgage-ready. [Learn more about credit readiness ->](/credit-readiness)

**Q3: How long does it take to buy a home from start to finish?**
A: Most of our first-time buyers go from planning session to keys in hand in 45-60 days. The mortgage process takes about 21-30 days once your offer is accepted. Getting pre-approved early speeds things up.
