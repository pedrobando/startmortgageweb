## Elementor Build Notes

- **One primary CTA per section.** "Book Your Free Planning Session" or "Book My Planning Session" is the conversion action. It appears in Sections 2, 4, 7, and 10.
- **Mobile:** Hero CTA should be sticky on mobile. Phone number always tappable.
- **Images:** Use real photos of Jexayra and real closings. Avoid stock.
- **Fonts:** Body text 16px minimum. H1 at 40px+.
- **Speed:** Compress all images (WebP preferred). Use Elementor lazy load. Above-the-fold load time under 3 seconds.
- **RankMath:** Set focus keyword to "mortgage broker kissimmee." Verify green SEO score. Add internal links as mapped above.
- **Accessibility:** Alt text on all images. Sufficient color contrast on CTAs. Form fields labeled.
