**Body Copy (Text Editor):**
A USDA loan is a home loan backed by the U.S. Department of Agriculture. It is designed for buyers in eligible rural and suburban areas.

Do not let the word "rural" fool you. Many neighborhoods near Kissimmee and across Central Florida qualify — places that look and feel like any other suburb.

Here is what makes USDA stand out:

Zero down payment. You finance the full purchase price. Mortgage insurance costs are lower than FHA. And rates are competitive because the USDA guarantees a portion of the loan.

There are income limits. And the home must be in a USDA-eligible area. But for buyers who qualify, this is one of the most affordable paths to homeownership.

As a mortgage broker, START Mortgage compares USDA options from multiple lenders. We check your eligibility, shop the rates, and find the fit.

### SECTION 7: FAQ Accordion (3 Items)

**Layout:** Accordion / toggle widget. Clean, simple. Light background.

**H2:**
Common Questions About USDA Loans in Central Florida

**FAQ 1:**
**Q: Is my area eligible for a USDA loan?**
A: Many suburban areas around Kissimmee and Central Florida are USDA-eligible — including places that do not look "rural" at all. The USDA has an online map that shows eligible areas. During your free planning session, we will check any address you are considering.

**FAQ 2:**
**Q: What are the income limits for a USDA loan?**
A: Income limits depend on your household size and the county where the home is located. The USDA sets these limits and updates them regularly. We will compare your household income to the current limits during your planning session. Many moderate-income families qualify.

**FAQ 3:**
**Q: Can I buy any type of home with a USDA loan?**
A: USDA loans can be used for single-family homes, including new construction in some cases. The home must be your primary residence — no investment properties or vacation homes. Condos and manufactured homes may qualify if they meet USDA standards. We will help you check.
