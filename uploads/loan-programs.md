**Q: How do I know which loan program is right for me?**

**FAQ 2:**
**Q: What is the difference between a mortgage broker and a lender?**
A: A lender funds your loan using their own products. A mortgage broker — like START Mortgage — connects you with multiple lenders and compares options on your behalf. You get one application, one point of contact, and access to more choices. We do the shopping. You make the decision.
