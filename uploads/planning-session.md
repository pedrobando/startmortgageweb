## Elementor Implementation Notes

- **Template:** Uses Inviz Contact template. Map sections 1-7 above to the corresponding Inviz Contact template sections (Hero, Appointment Form, Contact Info Cards, Google Maps, FAQ Accordion, CTA + Email Form, Footer).
- **This is the primary conversion page for the entire site.** Every other page funnels here. Treat it accordingly.
- **Booking embed** should be above the fold on desktop (right column of hero). On mobile, it appears directly below the hero body copy.
- **Sticky mobile CTA:** "Book Your Free Planning Session" button stays visible at bottom of screen on mobile at all times.
- **Page speed:** Keep this page fast. No heavy animations. Compress all images. Lazy-load everything below the fold.
- **Hero image/background:** Use a real photo — Jexayra on a call, a family smiling at a closing table, or a warm lifestyle image. No stock photos of people.
- **Accordion FAQ:** Use Elementor's native accordion widget. Keep answers concise — two to three sentences max. Set first item to closed (do not auto-open). Ensure FAQ content matches the FAQPage schema markup exactly.
- **CTA button color:** Use the same primary CTA color as the homepage for consistency. High contrast against section backgrounds.
- **One CTA per section.** "Book Your Free Planning Session" is the only action on this page.
- **RankMath:** Set focus keyword to "mortgage consultation kissimmee." Verify green SEO score. Confirm meta title and description render correctly in snippet preview.
- **301 redirect:** Configure `/mortgage-planning/` to 301 redirect to `/planning-session` in WordPress (via RankMath redirects or Redirection plugin).
- **Phone numbers:** Make all phone numbers click-to-call with `tel:` links. Format: `<a href="tel:6892103448">(689) 210-3448</a>`.
- **Tracking:** Add conversion event on booking confirmation (for GA4 and any paid ad pixels). This is the site's primary conversion event.
- **Accessibility:** All form fields must have proper labels. Map embed must have a descriptive title attribute.
