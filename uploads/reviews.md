## Elementor Notes

- **Template mapping:** Uses Inviz About template. Map sections as: Hero + Counters (1), Image Gallery (2), Executive Feature Block (3), Strategy Cards (4), 5 Team/Theme Cards (5), Team Gallery/Proof + Leave Review (6), CTA + Social (7), Email Subscription (8), Footer (9).
- **Let the proof do the talking.** Minimal copy between testimonials. Big quotes. Clean layout. White space.
- **Featured testimonial (Section 3):** Use large font size for quote text (20-24px). Subtle quote mark icons or background accents.
- **Theme cards (Section 5):** Keep short and punchy. One sentence per card. Theme labels reinforce what matters.
- **Strategy cards (Section 4):** Full testimonials with theme highlights beneath. Two-column layout.
- **Mobile:** Stack all cards vertically. Ensure quotes remain readable at mobile font sizes.
- **Images:** If client photos become available, add them to testimonial cards. Until then, use clean card design without placeholder stock photos.
- **Review link:** Replace Google Review CTA link with actual GBP review URL once listing is live under START Mortgage.
- **AggregateRating schema:** Includes individual Review objects for richer search results.
- **RankMath:** Focus keyword "start mortgage reviews." Verify green score.
